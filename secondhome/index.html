About the place

Second home is situated in Tortosa, Catalunya. We are now opened for weekends and vacation. We will soon start to offer our service for the week days and the school year. The house is situated in the center of Tortosa, not far from the parador. It includes all the necessary amenities:

  • A room for the children.

  • A big terasse
  • A huge natural place
